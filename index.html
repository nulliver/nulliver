My Kid Battery

Turn naptime into recharge time!

My Kid Battery lets you create fun low-battery photos of your child as a playful reminder when they need to rest and recharge.

Features
  • Take photos with your camera
  • Choose from 6 battery levels (10% – 100%)
  • Intelligent person detection automatically places the battery on your child
  • Drag, pinch, and rotate to perfect the placement
  • Save and share your creations
